Voting Instructions

The following instructions are meant to guide AGI token holders through the voting process of the SingularityNET Supervisory Council Election.
To learn more about the SingularityNET Supervisory Council, its functions, and the voting process, please read this blog post.

Note: For your vote to be considered, your AGI balance on the voting wallet cannot be moved during the voting period.

  1. Install Metamask
    • How to use Metamask
    • Connect your hardware wallet to Metamask (optional)
    • Import existing private key or JSON file to Metamask (optional)
  2. Be sure you have at least 1 AGI token in your Metamask/Hardware wallet for your vote to be considered (your vote will weight is determined according to your AGI balance)
  3. Click the ‚ÄúVOTE‚Äù button in front of your preferred 3 candidates (order matters)¬Ø
  4. Once you have selected your top 3 nominees, click ‚ÄúSIGN & CONFIRM‚Äù
  5. A Metamask pop-up will appear, click ‚ÄúSIGN‚Äù
  6. Do not move the AGI tokens in that wallet until the voting period is over


Notes

  1. The voting period will last for 24h and starts on June 12, 00:00 UTC.
  2. Token holders can participate by signing an Ethereum message that weights their vote according to the AGI balance on their voting wallet.
  3. Participants submit their vote for 3 candidates and the order in which they are selected will be considered for the election.
  4. Your vote will only be submitted once you click on ‚ÄúSIGN & CONFIRM‚Äù.
  5. After the voting period is over, there will be a blockchain audit to disqualify voters who have not kept their AGI tokens frozen. This ensures voters can only vote once with their entire AGI balance, preventing manipulation.
