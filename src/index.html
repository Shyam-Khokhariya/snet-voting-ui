Voting Instructions

The following instructions are meant to guide AGI token holders through the voting process on implementing SingularityNet phase two proposal.

To learn more about the phase two proposal and the reasons behind it, please read this blog post.

Important Note: For your vote to be considered, your AGI balance on the voting wallet cannot be moved between the time you place your vote and the end of the voting period.
  1. Install Metamask
    • How to use Metamask
    • Connect your hardware wallet to Metamask (optional)
    • Import existing private key or JSON file to Metamask (optional)
  2. Be sure you have at least 1 AGI token in your Metamask/Hardware wallet for your vote to be considered - your vote's weight is determined according to your AGI balance
  3. Click the "VOTE" button in front of your preferred option
  4. Once you have selected your option, click "SIGN & CONFIRM"
  5. A Metamask pop-up will appear, click “SIGN”
  6. Do not move the AGI tokens in that wallet until the voting period is over
Notes
  1. The voting period will last for 120hrs (96 hours vote is open + 36 hours blockchain audit) starting at 9 am on Wednesday the 3rd of February.
  2. It is imperative that once you have voted you do not move any tokens until after the voting period ends or you will invalidate your vote.
  3. Token holders can participate by signing a message with their Ethereum wallet that weights their vote according to the AGI balance on their voting wallet and any tokens staked using the voting wallet.
  4. Your vote will only be submitted once you click on "SIGN & CONFIRM".
  5. After the voting period is over, there will be a blockchain audit to disqualify voters who have not kept their AGI tokens frozen for during the voting period. This ensures voters can only vote once with their entire AGI balance, thus preventing manipulation.
